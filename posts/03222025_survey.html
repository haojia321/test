Survey Summary of Music Miracles Concert

March 22, 2025 at Brightview Great Falls By Anne Tang

A total of 10 attendees completed the survey regarding their experience at the Music Miracles concert held at Brightview Great Falls today. Below is a detailed summary of the survey results:

  1. Overall Concert Experience:
    • Excellent: 9 responses
    • Both Excellent and Good: 1 response
    • Fairly: 0 response
    • Poorly: 0 response

    We did Excellent mostly and got Excellent and Good from one person. The experience is amazingly better than last time. We should keep on making this good progress.

  2. Likelihood of Attending Another Music Miracles Concert:
    • Very Likely: 8 responses
    • Likely: 2 responses
    • Unlikely: 0 response
    • Very Unlikely: 0 response

    The audiences enjoyed the performances and many of them want to come back next time. They liked it a lot too.

  3. Favorite Types of Performances:
    • Classic Piano: 10 votes
    • Pop Piano: 3 votes
    • Violin: 6 votes
    • Piano and Vocal: 5 votes
    • Modern Style Dance: 2 votes
    • Gu Zheng: 2 votes

    Overall, everybody loved the Classic Piano. They also like Violin, Piano & Vocal, Pop Piano, Modern Style Dance, and Guzheng too.

  4. Enjoyed Aspects of the Concert:
    • Variety: 7 responses
    • Quality: 7 responses
    • Length: 2 responses
    • Atmosphere: 2 responses

    Many people liked the aspects: Variety, and Quality. The other aspects they liked were Length and Atmosphere.

  5. Performances Not Enjoyed:
    • None.
    • Did not understand some of the opera notes.
    • No.
    • All Were Absolutely Good.

    Next time, we should sing louder and clear. The songs are all wonderful, but the audience wants to hear clearly from the singer(s). That would make the performance better.

  6. Suggestions for Improvement and Additional Comments:
    • No.
    • Tell Us More About the Instrument Gu Zheng.
    • Microphone Was an Excellent Idea.
    • Very Enjoyable, Thank You Very Much.
    • All Good.
    • They Are Very Beautiful!

    Summarizing the comments, everyone liked it. Although, we should explain more about the Guzheng, a special Chinese musical instrument next time. One person appreciated specific aspects like the use of the microphone. The rest were liked by the audiences.
